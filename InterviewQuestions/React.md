## Question 1
What are some of the lifecycles of react?
What are some of the hooks?
<details>
  <summary>Answer</summary>

  1. Initialization
    - `getInitialState()`
    - `getDefaultProps()`
    - `componentWillMount()`
    - `render()`
    - `componentDidMount()`
  1. State/Propery Updates
    - `componentWillReceiveProps()`
    - `shouldComponentUpdate()`
    - `componentWillUpdate()`
    - `render()`
    - `componentDidUpdate()`
  1. Destruction
    - `componentWillUnmount()`
</details>
